OCTOBER 7, 2023

ISRAEL UNDER ATTACK

HAMAS LAUNCHES SURPRISE ASSAULT

In a sudden and unprecedented escalation, Hamas militants have launched a massive surprise attack on Israel from the Gaza Strip. Thousands of rockets have been fired, and ground incursions are reported in southern Israeli communities.

The Israeli government has declared a state of war as the IDF mobilizes reserves to respond to the crisis.
